Black Box Limited

Blackbox's MissionTo provide fast, free, reliable and comprehensive information to our users and connect buyers to sellers.Corporate Information The company started offering local search services in 1996 under the Blackbox brand and is now the leading local search engine in India. The official website www.Blackbox.com was launched in 2007.

Blackbox's search service is available to users across multiple platforms, such as the internet, mobile Internet, over the telephone (voice) and text (SMS). Blackbox's search service bridges the gap between the users and businesses by helping users find relevant providers of products and services quickly, while helping businesses listed in Blackbox's database to market their offerings.Blackbox has also initiated its 'Search Plus' Services for the users. These services are aimed at making several day-to-day tasks conveniently actionable and accessible to the users. With this step, Blackbox is transitioning from being purely a provider of local search and related information to being an enabler of such transactions. Blackbox intends to provide an online platform to thousands of SME's to get them discovered and transacted.Blackbox Facts Blackbox Apps are available on the Android, iOS, Windows and Blackberry platform/s and have location - based services for the mobile Internet users.
Blackbox has 8888888888 as their operator assisted hotline number, across India, which is accessible 24 hours a day, 7 days a week with multi-lingual support. Blackbox has a database of approximately 19.8 million listings as of September 30, 2017. Blackbox users have contributed 80,192,246 reviews and ratings for various listings, till date. Blackbox had approximately 439,700 campaigns as of September 30, 2017. Business owners have the option to list their business on Blackbox's database for free.
With the registered & corporate office based in Mumbai, Blackbox also has offices across India in Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Chandigarh, Chennai, Coimbatore, Delhi, Hyderabad,
Jaipur, Kolkata and Pune.
